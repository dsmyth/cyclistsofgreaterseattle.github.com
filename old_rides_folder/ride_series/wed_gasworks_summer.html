Ride Description:
     This is a mid-week training ride for those who live or work in Seattle. We will vary the routes from week to week and sample Seattle’s neighborhoods, parks and waterfronts. We will climb at least one or two hills to strengthen our lungs and legs, but the ultimate goal will be to catch sunsets from high and low. There will be an optional get-together at the establishment of our choice after the ride.
     Headlights & taillights recommended for safety.

Gasworks Wednesday Ride: Training Series

Ride Days: Wednesday Evenings.
Ride Start: Gasworks Park, Seattle .
Meet At: 6:15 pm, start 6:30pm sharp.
Ride Pace: Brisk.
Ride Distance: 2-35 Miles.
Ride Terrain: Mixed / Hilly.Ride Leads: Presenting... Arnie, Bill, Charlie, Dan, Garry, Garth, Gary , Howard, Jimmy, Louise, Martha, Ron L, Ron T, Stacey, Tom, et al.
Rain Policy: Heavy rain cancels.
Ride Description:

Get geared up for Winter Rides! We will be singing in the rain and splashing through puddles. Get lights and rain gear, and we will ride into the night. Ride leaders will rotate and take the group to their favorite haunts. We will look for glimmering city lights and full moon on trails. Afterwards, we will slurp down hot soups and huddle close for warmth and conversation at an establishment of our choice.

Headlights & taillights required for safety durinng winter months.

 Saturday, December 15, 2007 Jack Block Park SeaTac Loop
Ride Start:  Jack Block Park in West Seattle 2130 Harbor Ave (just before Salty's)
Meet At:  945 a.m. at the start and begin at 10 a.m. sharp.
Ride Pace:  Moderate/Brisk (14-17 mph) but will slow down on hills to regroup and if weather dictates a slower pace.
Ride Distance: 35 or so miles but we might tweak this distance up or down
Ride Terrain: Rolling hills - plus a few steep ones
Ride Leader: Gary Strauss (garystrauss@gmail.com.com) (206-816-1300 during week; 206-948-9024 day of ride)
Rain Policy: Rain, ice or snow cancels.
Regrouping:  Will regroup on the top of hills and at various other points.  We may not ride that hard. Depending on the weather, we may alter the course so we do expect to stay together when we can.
Cue Sheet:    Yes
Ride Description:  This is a slice of south Seattle and the communities in South King County.  A somewhat hilly loop through the not usually taken back roads of West Seattle to Burien to SeaTac to South Park. No planned rest stops. Lunch/coffee optional at the end at Alki Bakery or another place (adds about 4 miles). Not plan to push too hard (can't keep up with the mountain goats anyway). Come prepared with at least 2 new tubes, a patch kit, and a pump or CO2 cartridges. Please check your tires before leaving home.  Note: if weather turns bad during ride, we will consider a number of bail out options.

Ride Leaders: Jim and Vickie Stewart jim.stewart@manatron.com. Day of ride call Jim’s cell: 206-890-8411

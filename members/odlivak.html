Background

Victor was actually obsessed with bicycles long before this incarnation.

In fact as Klaus Seckel in the Netherlands who wrote a diary, Der Taegesbuch der Klaus Seckel there is an intimate description of him changing a flat tire one day.

"Since 1985 my only vehicles have been bicycles, though I have been known to use Taxi's and take rides from friends. Any bike in any form is beautiful, from recumbent to Moulton to mountain bike to a Brompton folder. Yes I have had all these and more. I enjoy leading rides that are sociable and involve a lot of eating and getting to know the local culture. The last ride I led took 13 days and 550 km in Cuba. So remember Compañeros as riding a bicycle is a revolutionary act, and the revolution will not be televized!" - Victor -

Club Volunteering

Since the club opened in 1998, Victor has been the unofficial Director of Postal Services stopping at the Post Office each week to pick up the club mail. Officially this year Victor is the Membership Coordinator and assists by maintaining the membership database and welcoming new members. While Victor is a very strong cyclist he prefers to cycle at a slower pace and has quite a following on his leisure/social rides that are much more fun than exercise.

Background

I started biking in about 1980 with the Sierra Club in the Bay Area.  Most of our rides, pleasantly enough, were in the wine country!  I've been in Seattle since 1991 and began biking with fellow bikers in a local ski club.  I've led rides with the ski club and most recently (last summer) have led some Escargot Rides for SBC from Gas Works Park on Thursday nights.  So now my secret is out, I'm not a power rider, but a leisurely or social rider and love it!

I look forward to being secretary of SBC and hope this will allow me to get acquainted with more members!

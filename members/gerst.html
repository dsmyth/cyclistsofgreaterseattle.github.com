Background

I didn’t begin biking until about 15 years ago and then only at the insistence of a friend. Found out I loved it and wanted to bike everywhere. So I started planning weekend tours for friends around the area. When that wasn’t enough to satisfy my touring desire I signed up for weeklong tours with Cycle America and most recently Tour BC. In fact, I joined Seattle Bike Club so I could participate in the touring weekend that was held at Birch Bay.

You probably won’t see me on many rides in the winter, as I am not much of a winter biker. I like to spend my winter weekends in the mountains cross-country skiing or snow shoeing.

Club Volunteering

I have been a ride leader and in charge of the kitchen for the potluck at the monthly meetings. I have organized one weekend tour for the club and hope to organize many more this year as the tour coordinator. If you would like to help with the tours or have any suggestions for tours please email me.

Background

Urania Perez has been bike commuting to work since 1996. She started first with the bike-to-work event, then every Friday and then she got addicted to be in contact with nature on the Burke Gilman Trail and being able to both commute by bike and exercise all at the same time.

Currently, Urania rides every day from Wedgwood to the Key Tower where she is employed by the City of Seattle as an environmental analyst.

Club Volunteering

Urania enjoys assisting other new bike commuters to determine what is the best route, gear to wear in the rain, lights, etc. She also has volunteered before with both the Seattle Bicycle Club and Cascade Bike Club. Contact her with questions about commuting.

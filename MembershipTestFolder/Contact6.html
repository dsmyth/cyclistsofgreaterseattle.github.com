Cyclists of Greater Seattle (COGS) is a club that is run by and for its members. COGS has no paid staff and encourages all of its members to assist with club functions and activities. COGS does not discriminate nor condone discrimination either because of race, color, creed, religion, sex, age, national origin, sexual orientation, marital status or veteran status.
COGS expects all of its members to wear helmets on its rides and act in a safe manner in accordance with local motor vehicle laws.

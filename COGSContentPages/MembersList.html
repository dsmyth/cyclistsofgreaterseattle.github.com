C.O.G.S. Members List Guidelines



COGS Members List is an email list that is exclusively for members of the. Use it to keep informed of new trips, deadlines for signup, meeting announcements and club news.

New members are automatically added to the list, unless they may opt out on the membership application

List Use Guidelines:


    * Please remember that posts will go to all members of the list.
    * Please refrain from issues not related to Biking or other club activities.
    * Please avoid profanity.
    * Because of the rapid and informal nature of communicating via E-mail, it is easy to be misunderstood.
    * Civility is best maintained by thoughtful postings and patient replies.
    * Try to be considerate of people's time and the community's resources.
    * Participants who persist in disrupting the list will be removed.

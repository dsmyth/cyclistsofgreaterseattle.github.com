COGS Social Events

Come and see if you can recognize COGS members without their helmets!

COGS is about riding and developing and making friendships. To encourage camaraderie on and off the bike, COGS hosts several social events each year. Events planned for this year include:

  • April potluck to kick off the cycling season
  • Summer evening picnics at Green Lake
  • Potluck Meeting in October
  • Holiday Party in December

Monthly Board Meeting

All members are invited to the board meetings which are generally held monthly.
The time, place, and agenda will be announced in an e-mail sent to the membership.
All members are welcome.

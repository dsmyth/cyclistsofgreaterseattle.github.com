May 18, 19, 20 Potholes Reservoir and Columbia Wildlife Refuge

Spend 3 days cycling and sightseeing in one of the most interesting and spectacular areas of sunny Eastern Washington.

 

Friday Ride

Time: 9:30

Meet: MarDon Resort

Distance: 45 miles

Pace: Social and Moderate

Ride Terrain: Mostly flat with a few hills

Details: We will ride through the Wildlife Refuge to Othello for lunch.

 

Saturday Ride

Time: 9:30

Meet: MarDon Resort

Distance: 45 miles

Pace: Moderate

Ride Terrain: Mostly flat

Ride Description: We will ride west and have lunch in Royal City

 

Sunday Ride or Hike in Wildlife Refuge

Time: 9:30

Meet: MarDon Resort

Distance: 35 miles

Pace: Moderate

Ride Terrain: Mostly flat with a few hills

Ride Description: We will stop in Ellensburg for a ride on the way back to Seattle. Or we can do a hike in the Wildlife Refuge.

Directions: Potholes Reservoir and MarDon Resort is located approximately 3 hours from Seattle.

June 23-26 The Dalles and Goldendale

Bike in the beautiful Columbia Gorge.  Make a visit to Stonehedge and Maryhill Museum and enjoy live music at Maryhill Winery 1-5.

Saturday: Goldendale

Time: 9:00 
Meet: Ponderosa Motel in Goldendale
Distance: 40 miles
Pace: Moderate (14-16 mph)
Ride Terrain: Some hills
Ride Description: See historic barns, old homesteads and the gentle rolling hills of beautiful Pleasant Valley.

Sunday: Loop ride in The Dalles

Time: 9:00 Drive 45 min to The Dalles to start ride
Meet: The Dalles Thompson Park
Distance: 40 miles
Pace: Moderate (14-16 mph)
Ride Terrain: Gradual climbing in the beginning
Ride Description:  We will do a loop ride starting in The Dalles heading south.

Monday: Along the Klickitat River

Time:  9:00 start (45 min drive from Goldendale to Lyle)
Meet: In Lyle at Intersection of Hwy 14 and 142
Distance: 40 miles
Pace: Moderate (14-16 mph)
Ride Terrain: Mostly flat
Ride Description: We will drive to Lyle for the start of the ride. It is a gorgeous out and back ride running along the Klickitat River. There is a store in Klickitat to buy some food but you should bring some lunch.

Tuesday Ride: Goldendale

Time:  9:00 start
Meet: Ponderosa Motel
Distance: 25 mile
Pace: Moderate (14-16 mph)
Ride Terrain: Rolling
Ride Description: A ride around Goldendale area before returning to Seattle.

 

 Lodging: Ponderosa Motel in Goldendale                    509-773-5842           509-773-5842

Camping: Maryhill State Park takes reservations.

Tour Coordinator: Mary Jo at maryjo1532@hotmail.com

July 27, 28, 29 San Juan Islands

The San Juan Islands are a bicycling paradise with lots of low traffic roads with great views.  Join us for 3 days of island hopping and great biking.


Friday Ride: Friday Harbor
Ride start: Start time approx. 1:30
Meet: Meet at the hostel
Ride Distance:  40 miles 
Ride Description:  We will ride out to Roche Harbor and around the island.
Details: Take the Ferry from Anacortes to Friday Harbor.  We will drop our bags at the hostel, have lunch in Friday Harbor and then do a moderate paced ride around the Island.  There is a grocery store on the main street in town for a sandwich for lunch and food for the weekend.

Saturday Ride: Orcas Island
Ride start: 8:45 ferry from Friday Harbor
Distance: 40 miles
Ride Description: We will ride to Eastsound Village to the outdoor Saturday Market and then on to Deer Harbor. Then back to the ferry dock for our return to Friday Harbor.

Sunday Ride:  Lopez Island
Ride start: 8:10 ferry from Friday Harbor
Distance: 33 miles
Ride Description:  We will ride around Lopez, the flattest of all the islands.  We will have lunch in town and then take the 3:25 ferry back to Anacortes, or Friday Harbor for those that want to spend an extra night.

 

Lodging: We will be staying at the Wayfarers Rest in Friday Harbor. This is a very nice hostel just 5 blocks from the ferry dock with kitchen facilities. They have private rooms available in addition to a small dorm. Book soon since lodging is scarce and fills up fast in the Islands.  See their website for details and reservations.  
Use the COGS email if you need a roommate or want to carpool. 
Tour Coordinator: Mary Jo at maryjo1532@hotmail.com

 

 Details: Leave your car at the upper parking lot at the ferry dock in Anacortes. Parking fee in Anacortes is 25.00 for 3 days.  The only ferry charge is going over to Friday Harbor.

 

August 17, 18, 19 Elma, Ocean Shores, Olympia

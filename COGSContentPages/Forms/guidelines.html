                               Members List Guidelines

SBC Members List is an email list that is exclusively for members of the
Seattle Bicycle Club. Keep informed of new trips, deadlines for signup,
meeting announcements and club news.

New members are automatically added to the list, unless they opt out on the
membership application

The SBC use guidelines are:

*    Please remember that posts will go to all members of the list.
*    Please refrain from issues not related to Biking or other club
activities.
*    Please avoid profanity.
*    Because of the rapid and informal nature of communicating via
E-mail, it is easy to be misunderstood.
*    Civility is best maintained by thoughtful postings and patient
replies.
*    Try to be considerate of people's time and the community's
resources.
*    Participants who persist in disrupting the list will be removed.

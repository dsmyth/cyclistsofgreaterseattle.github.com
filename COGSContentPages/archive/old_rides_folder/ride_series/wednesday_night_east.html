Wednesdays, Eastside Evening Escape Series
Ride Start: Marymoor Parking Lot K. ($1 Parking Fee.)
Start At: 6:30pm.
Ride Pace: High Moderate.
Ride Distance: 18-25 Miles.
Ride Terrain: Some Hills.
Rain Policy: Rain cancels.
Ride Lead: ChrisM, mackayc @ exponent.com 425-922-7435.                   or Mark Peterson, treasurer @ seattlebicycle.org
Ride Details:
     Come join us for our traditional Eastside Exercise Series created for those working or living on the Eastside who don't want to fight bridge traffic to attend a mid-week ride in Seattle. We will always meet at Marymoor Park but will vary the route each week.
     There will always be a post-ride get-together in Redmond.

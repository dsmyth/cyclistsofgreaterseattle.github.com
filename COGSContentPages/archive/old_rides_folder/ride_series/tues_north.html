July 26th, Tues.  North End Tuesday Ride Series

Ride Start: Richmond Beach Rd and NW 8th St. parking lot of the QFC by Starbucks
Meet At: 6:15 pm, start 6:30 pm sharp.
Ride Pace: Moderate.
Ride Distance: 18-20 Miles.
Ride Terrain: Mostly flat, some hills.
Ride Info: Mary Jo 206-546-0645 maryjo1532 @ hotmail.com
Ride Leads: Mary Jo, MarkB, et al.
Rain Policy: Heavy rain cancels.
Ride Description:
  For those of us who live or work in the North End. We will do varied loops around the
area on lightly traffic roads, enjoying water and mountain views.  Starting place may
vary during the summer. No ride the third Tuesday of the month. There will be an optional
get-together at the establishment of our choice after the ride.

Ride Days: Most Saturdays from March to August unless otherwise posted.
Ride Start: See Below.
Meet At: See Below.
Ride Pace: High Moderate to Brisk depending on terrain, number of riders, availability of leaders, etc. On certain rides, we may offer different paces with different leaders.
Ride Distance: See Below.
Elevation Gain: See Below.
Ride Terrain: Hilly to Mountainous.
Ride Coordinator: Mark P treasurer @ seattlebicycle.org

Ride Leaders: See Below.

Rain Policy: Heavy rain cancels. Contact ride leader for verification if weather seems uncertain.

Ride Description:
     This training series will prepare riders for RAMROD and other endurance rides. The rides will increase gradually in distance, elevation gain and difficulty. Ride start times and locations will vary from week to week and may change to accommodate current weather conditions, so please check the website before each ride for confirmation. Participants should bear in mind that, while there may be re-group points along the way, the ride is unsupported. Each rider must be capable of meeting the physical demands of planned routes. Cue sheets will be provided. All rides will begin promptly on time, so please be at the start location at least 15 minutes before the start time to sign in.  There are several event rides listed in the schedule that can be used as training rides.  As the dates get closer and leaders volunteer, local alternative rides may be added.

     For more information on Ramrod and instructions on how to get signed up see the the Redmond Cycling Club website website.
     Bicycle flashers and bright clothing recommended.

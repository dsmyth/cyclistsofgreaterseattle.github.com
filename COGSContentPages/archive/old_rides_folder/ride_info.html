Ride Information

All riders must wear helmets on Seattle Bicycle Club Rides and are expected to obey the rules of the road, including showing proper etiquette towards cars, bikes and pedestrians. Riders who do not obey traffic rules will be asked to leave.

Please bring the following along on all rides: Spare tube and/or patch kit; pump; ID; $ for food; water! ID should specify emergency contact name and phone number, and rider blood type and a list of any allergies and/or specific medical needs. All riders under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or responsible adult.

We encourage you to arrive at the start 15 minutes before the ride, to sign-in, review the ride map and prep your bicycle. If you have any questions about the ride, ask the Ride Leader or one of the Ride Leader Assistants before the ride begins -- they are there to help and advise.

Our regular club rides are intended for members and their guests, and for out-of-town visitors to Seattle. We do, though, welcome our fellow Puget Sounders to take a club ride to audition us -- we're sure if you ride with us, you'll want to be part of our club.

Those who participate in Weekend Touring Events must be SBC members.

Ride Legend
Ride Paces (Regroups)
Social - 12-14 mph (frequent)
Moderate - 14-16 mph (regular)
Brisk - 16-18 (occasional)

Ride paces are intended to be averages for the flats. Regardless of pace, we regroup at the top of big or long hills. Faster riders are welcome to ride ahead, but they won't set the pace.

Weather conditions vary drastically throughout the area and what may concern one ride leader may not another. If you have any questions concerning the status of a ride, be sure to contact the ride coordinator.
